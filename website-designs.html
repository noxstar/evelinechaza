Course: CMPT 370- Software Engineering A software application developed for group expense tracking and splitting, automating the distribution of bills, receipts, and costs among participants. Developed using React Framework (NextJS), Tailwind CSS, and SQLite Embedded Databases. Applied agile principles to ensure an adaptive development process. This was a group project of six people. Spearheaded the development of the "settle up" feature, allowing users to effortlessly make payments, dynamically update the database, and ensure real-time adjustments to individual balances.
